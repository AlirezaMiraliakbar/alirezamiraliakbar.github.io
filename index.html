I am PhD student in Chemical Engineering at University of Connecticut. I am member of the Process Systems and Operations Research (PSOR) Lab under the supervison of Prof. Matthew Stuber My research interests include Process Optimization, Machine Learning and Software Development for Chemical Industries.

My work focuses on optimization, computational modeling, and the development of tools that bridge theory and application in chemical engineering.
